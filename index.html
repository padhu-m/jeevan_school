Education

The purpose of education is to develop the student’s out of the box thinking skills and the ability to learn and understand.

Mission

Vision

Correspondents Message

Dr. Abdul Azeez

Correspondent

‘Knowledge is Power’ and it is in our hands to shape our destiny, in the belief that “Good education is the highway to personal and national success” the school aims at bringing out what is best in the wholesome of development of child, ie., the physical, intellectual and moral developments. It aims to instill in the child, through relevant learning experiences and opportunities, a desire to learn, not just in the classroom but all the time, making it a lifelong learning process.
